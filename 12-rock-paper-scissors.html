Rock Paper Scissors

How to Play

Welcome to the classic game of Rock Paper Scissors! Follow these simple instructions to play:

Game Rules

  • Rock beats Scissors: Rock crushes Scissors.
  • Scissors beats Paper: Scissors cut Paper.
  • Paper beats Rock: Paper covers Rock.

How to Play

  1. Select Your Move:
    • Click the Rock button to choose Rock.
    • Click the Paper button to choose Paper.
    • Click the Scissors button to choose Scissors.
  2. View the Result: After making your selection, the computer will randomly choose its move. The result of the game will be displayed, indicating whether you won, lost, or tied.
  3. Check Your Score: Your wins, losses, and ties will be displayed on the screen, keeping track of your performance.

Additional Features

  • Reset Score: Click the "Reset Score" button to reset your wins, losses, and ties to zero.
  • Auto Play: Click the "Auto Play" button to let the computer automatically play the game for you. This can be a fun way to see random results quickly.

Enjoy the Game!

Whether you play against the computer or let it auto-play, have fun and try to beat the computer with your strategic choices!
